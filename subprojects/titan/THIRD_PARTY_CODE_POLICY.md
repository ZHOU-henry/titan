# Third-Party Code Policy

## Rules

1. Do not import code, models, datasets, or safety materials without clear licensing.
2. Keep provenance records for all reused third-party materials.
3. Preserve attribution, copyright, and notice obligations.
4. Escalate any GPL/AGPL/LGPL or unclear-license dependency before use.
5. Use clean-room reimplementation when the source license is unclear or operationally risky.

Open-source intent and delayed monetization do not remove license obligations.
